# Bus travel questionnaire — results

## Study background

Short online questionnaire run to understand how people plan and catch buses today and where the experience breaks down. Distributed through community channels and a transit operator's mailing list.

- **Field dates:** 2026-05-12 to 2026-05-26
- **Responses:** 142 complete
- **Format:** mix of single-choice, 5-point agreement scales, and one open text question
- **Respondent profile:** adults who took the bus at least once in the last month

Percentages are rounded and may not sum to exactly 100. Counts are out of 142 unless noted.

## Closed questions

### Q1. How do you usually check when your bus will arrive?

| Method | Count | % |
|---|---|---|
| A transit app on my phone | 55 | 39% |
| Printed timetable at the stop | 38 | 27% |
| Real-time display at the stop | 26 | 18% |
| I just show up and wait | 17 | 12% |
| Ask someone / other | 6 | 4% |

### Q2. What is your single biggest frustration with catching the bus?

| Frustration | Count | % |
|---|---|---|
| Not knowing if the bus is actually coming | 44 | 31% |
| Bus arrived late or early vs. the schedule | 37 | 26% |
| The real-time information was wrong | 24 | 17% |
| Bus was too crowded / full | 13 | 9% |
| Confusing routes or connections | 11 | 8% |
| Ticketing or payment | 9 | 6% |
| Other | 4 | 3% |

### Q3. What would most improve your bus experience? (pick one)

| Improvement | Count | % |
|---|---|---|
| Accurate, real-time arrival information | 67 | 47% |
| More frequent / more reliable service | 30 | 21% |
| A personal app with saved routes & notifications | 20 | 14% |
| Simpler tickets / payment | 13 | 9% |
| Better signage & timetables at stops | 8 | 6% |
| Other | 4 | 3% |

## Open question

### Q7. "What is the one thing you would change about catching the bus?"

96 of 142 respondents left a free-text answer. Responses were coded into themes; a single answer could be counted under more than one theme, so mentions do not sum to 96.

| Theme | Mentions |
|---|---|
| Just want to know it is actually coming / show up with confidence | 39 |
| Real-time predictions are often wrong / cannot trust them | 28 |
| Hate waiting with no information, especially in bad weather | 22 |
| Wish there were a good app / a better app | 18 |
| Disruptions and cancellations are not communicated | 16 |
| Fares or tickets are confusing or expensive | 11 |
| Accessibility (steps, space, info for impaired riders) | 7 |

### Sample verbatim responses (unedited)

- "Honestly I don't need anything fancy. Just tell me the truth about when it's coming."
- "The app says 3 minutes for ten minutes straight. I've stopped believing it."
- "A proper app that remembers my routes and pings me when to leave would change my life."
- "Standing in the rain not knowing if I've already missed it is the worst part of my day."
- "If it's cancelled, tell me. I'll find another way. Just don't leave me guessing."
- "More buses, more often. That's the whole problem really."
- "I'd use an app if it actually worked. The ones I've tried are wrong as often as the sign."
